The Tavern

The Tavern is Borderland Keep's Mastodon instance. Mastodon is a federated version of mircorblogging platforms like twitter or threads. Sign up just like you would on any of those platforms, howver now you have access to any other mastodon instance that is being hosted. No need to make multiple accounts for Borderland Keep, Dice Camp or anyt other instance. You cross post and interact with them to your hearts content.
